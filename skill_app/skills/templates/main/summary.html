How to Use

1. Create profile and enter each skill

2. Jobs are matched based on skill match percentage

3. Apply by clicking 'apply'
Hiring App is focused around using specific skills as blocks to determine job fit.

1. Profile Setup

Each user first enters in specific skills and the length of time they have worked in that skill.

This is done once, and as needed when new skills are learned.

2. Smart Match

Job positions are analyzed based on which skills are a good match.

Location, salary, and a variety of pre-set preferences are also accounted for.
This takes the focus off of searching for one specific job title, and opens up many possibilities.

3. Apply

Simply click the 'apply' button, and that's it!

A message is sent to the company, and they will follow up within 5 business days.
Gone are the days of sifting through hundreds of out-dated, irrelevant job postings.
Hiring app helps the candidate find the perfect job fit, and the company the perfect employee.
